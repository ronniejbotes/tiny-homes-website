### Cost bases & notes

- **Bathroom pod** — Imported integrated GRP/SMC pod (shower, toilet, basin, LED, extractor) FOB China ≈ $1 300 ≈ R24 000 @ R18.50/USD; +45% shipping+duties (per landed-cost rule) ≈ R35 000 landed ex VAT; + ~R4 500 on-site plumbing/electrical connection = R39 500. Cross-check: SA site-built small bathroom runs R30 000–R66 000 (procompare, localpros), so a R56 500 factory pod sits inside the plausible band. SA supplier GEZA sells an equivalent "Bathroom Cabin" but is POA. **Big-ticket add-on flag:** R56 500 is >100% of the R54 950 folding-home base price — present it as a major upgrade there. Not offered on nature-cabins/apple-cabins/glamping-capsules (bathroom already included; those get finish upgrades instead).
- **Kitchenette 2.4–3 m** — Mid-range melamine cabinetry R4 000–R8 000/lm supplied+installed (cupboarddoctor, kitchenpros); trade cost ~R5 700/lm × 2.7 m ≈ R15 500 incl postform counter, + sink & mixer ~R2 500, + install labour ~R3 000 (R1 500–R4 000/day, buildersingauteng), + plumbing connection ~R1 000 → R23 000.
- **Overhead cupboards** — Flat-pack 600 mm wall units retail R659–R967 (Modukitchen via Leroy Merlin); fitted-quality melamine units ~R1 800–R2 200 each supplied; 3 units + fixing labour → R7 000.
- **Smart lock** — ZKTeco ML100 fingerprint/keypad/WiFi from R2 860; comparable Takealot units R3 499; handyman/locksmith fit R800–R1 200 in Gauteng → R3 800 all-in. Standard already on apple-cabins and glamping-capsules.
- **LED ambient package** — 2 × 5 m RGB/smart strips (Leroy Merlin 5 m RGB from R476; smart variants ~R800), 6 dimmable downlights ~R1 500, smart dimmer/controller ~R800, electrician ~half day (downlight install from R180/point at Leroy Merlin services) ≈ R1 600 → R5 500. The Dome already includes an LED+Bluetooth system as standard.
- **Blackout curtain set** — 4–6 ready-made 100% blockout panels R400–R800 each (Mr Price Home, Leroy Merlin), 2–3 tracks/rods R300–R600 each, fitting ~R700 → R5 000 per unit. Made-to-measure alternative runs ~USD 40–80/m² installed (IndexBox), i.e. R6 000–R12 000 for a glass-heavy unit — quote separately for glamping-capsules if custom fabric is wanted. The Dome ships with blackout curtains standard (upgrade path there is the electric track).
